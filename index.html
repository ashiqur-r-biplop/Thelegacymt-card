Meal Services

We handle the preparation of daily meals for our residents as well as snacks throughout the day. Through our personalized care plans, we deliver accommodations for special dietary needs for residents.

Daily Housekeeping

Our staff provides daily housekeeping to make beds, clean bathrooms and tidy up resident rooms.

Daily Exercise Class

Regular exercise can help boost energy, maintain independence, and manage symptoms of illness or pain. And not only is exercise good for your body, it’s also good for your mind, mood, and memory.

Laundry Services

Scheduled weekly laundry services for residents' clothes and linens.

Medication Management

We partner with local pharmacies to make sure our residents have access to the medications they need. Our staff assists our residents by providing reminders and prompting to let them know when medication needs to be taken.

Personalized Care Plans

Scheduled weekly laundry services for residents' clothes. Some of our residents have unique needs, and we are willing to discuss and create plans to accommodate those needs.

Schedule Transportation

Residents can schedule local transportation with our staff for personal or group outings.

​Emergency Call System

Each resident is provided with a safety pendant that provides the ability to call for the assistance of our staff.

24-hour Staffing

We provide 24-hour staffing so that our residents needs are met around the clock.
